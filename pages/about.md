This site's contents are based on proposals developed by the [TL4BSSw Group](pages/workgroup.md). It is informed by the expertise and experience of this group in addition to existing theoretical and empirical work on team learning and scientific software. Content will be updated based on emerging work on team learning in collaborative scientific software projects.

External contributions are welcome. If you are interested in contributing and/or opportunities to connect with the TL4BSSw workshop group, please contact Olivia B. Newton via onewton[at]sigsoft.org.
