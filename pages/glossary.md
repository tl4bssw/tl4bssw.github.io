```{glossary}
Scientific software
:   "Software with a large computational component. Further, scientific software is usually developed by multidisciplinary teams made up of scientists and software developers." (<a href = "https://doi.org/10.1016/j.infsof.2014.05.006">Kanewala & Bieman, 2014</a>); "Software that aids in research, testing or design of scientific models that are used to explain and predict the behavior of real objects or systems in a variety of scientific disciplines." (<a href = "https://publish.tntech.edu/index.php/PSRCI/article/view/679">Shakya et al., 2020</a>)
```
